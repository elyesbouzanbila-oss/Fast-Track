about the app

Why We Created This App

We developed this platform to address the ongoing transportation challenges in our country. Public transit can often be unpredictable, delayed, or confusing, making daily travel stressful for many people.

Our goal is to solve these everyday issues by providing clear, real-time information about buses, trains, and other public transportation options.

This app is designed for every Tunisian who relies on public transport, helping you plan your journeys efficiently, avoid delays, and stay informed about disruptions or changes in service.

By using this app, we hope to make commuting easier, safer, and more predictable for everyone.
